# Work

## Company
**Redemption Home Remodeling (RHR)**
Residential design-and-build company. Focus on larger, higher-end remodels.

## Business Model
- Project-based. Projects have multiple phases (design → build).
- Joel handles sales and design phase, hands off to Caleb when customer is ready to build.
- Target project size: $30k–$100k+
- House flips are done occasionally to fill the schedule but are not the ideal project type.

## Services
- Full home remodels
- Kitchen remodels
- Bathroom remodels
- General residential construction

## Key Numbers Tracked
- Total revenue
- Profit margin
- Projects finishing on schedule
- Projects finishing without callbacks (first 4 weeks post-completion)

## Strategic Priority — Wildly Important Goal (WIG)
**Take operational success from 65% to 80% by end of 2026.**

Methodology: 4DX (Four Disciplines of Execution)

**Operational success is defined as:** a project is complete on schedule AND has zero callbacks in the first 4 weeks after completion. Both conditions must be met.

Current baseline: 65%
Target: 80%
Deadline: End of 2026
