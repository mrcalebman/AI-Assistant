## Summary
The video covers how to complete projects on schedule and without rework or callbacks. The central argument is that defining a clear finish line before work begins aligns the team around a shared definition of "done." Callbacks and rework are identified as the primary margin killers in project-based work. A pre-closeout inspection checklist is presented as the most actionable way to prevent callbacks.

## Key Points
- Set clear milestones upfront so the team knows what "done" looks like before starting
- Callbacks and rework are the biggest killers of project margin
- Inspect your own work before the customer does
- A final walkthrough checklist before closeout can cut callbacks by 50% or more
