## Employee Conduct

- Respect for one another and clients
- Honesty in all tasks, seen or unseen
- Accountability with time, tools, and company resources
- Growth mindset — own mistakes, learn from them
- Professionalism on jobsites and in client communication
- Protection of client privacy and respect for their homes
- Positive, solution-oriented attitude at all times
- No smoking, alcohol, or drugs
- No disrespectful/offensive communication or actions
- No harassment of any kind

### Being Good House Guests
- Notify customer ~24 hours in advance
- Notify again when 30 minutes away
- Knock and introduce yourself: "Hi, I'm [name] with Redemption Restorations..."
- Remove shoes or wear coverings; lay drop cloths from entrance to work area
- Close door behind you every time
- Don't damage walls, doors, or furniture with materials/tools
- End of day: pick up drop cloths, sweep and Swiffer visible dirt/dust
- Never ask the customer to do something you can do
- Show homeowner what was completed; give thank-you card on last visit

### Dust Control
- Drop cloths from work area to entry/exit
- Remove and photograph personal items in work area before starting
- Close off work area with plastic or door (towel at bottom)
- Fan in window for negative airflow if possible
- Cut materials in work area or outdoors — never near customer's personal items
- End of day: sweep, dust, vacuum, Swiffer work area; return personal items exactly as photographed

### Dress Code
- Facial hair must be trimmed and neat
- No pants with holes
- Redemption Restorations shirt or sweatshirt required inside customer's home
- Jeans or work pants only — no sweatpants or athletic shorts

## Communication

### Responsiveness Policy

| Recipient | Channel | Deadline |
|---|---|---|
| Internal | Email | 24 hours |
| Internal | Phone | 12 hours |
| Internal | Text | 24 hours |
| External (customers, subs) | Email | 12 hours |
| External | Phone | 3 hours |
| External | Text | 12 hours |

*Does not apply on weekends, vacation, or holidays.*

### Core Principles
- Verify facts before sharing. Use clear, complete sentences. Document client-facing decisions, changes, and approvals in writing (Houzz Pro, email, or text).
- Keep communication concise but complete. Use designated platforms for specific purposes.
- Treat every interaction as an opportunity to build trust. Respectful tone at all times. No profanity, sarcasm, or confrontational language.
- Listen first, then respond. Admit mistakes quickly. Invite feedback.

### Confidentiality
- Do not share company, client, or sub info outside the org without GM approval
- No pricing, project details, or client feedback in public spaces or social media
- Use company email and approved tools for all business correspondence

### Conflict Resolution
- Direct, respectful conversation first
- If unresolved, involve supervisor or GM
- Never discuss conflicts with clients or other team members outside your direct report chain

## Managing Company Resources

### Company Credit Card
- Business expenses only — no personal spending
- Submit receipts within one week of each transaction
- Receipt submission: Email to redemption_restorations+expenses@assist.intuit.com
  - Subject: [Job address] - [Transaction category] (e.g., "123 Main St - Materials")
  - Attach PDF or JPEG of receipt showing all transaction details
- Failure to submit receipts → removal of card, termination, or legal action
- Personal use → grounds for termination and legal action

### Company Vehicles
- Work-related purposes only (unless Director of Construction approves otherwise)
- Valid U.S. driver's license required
- No smoking, drinking, texting, or unsafe behavior
- Fuel with company credit card; follow receipt procedures
- Report accidents and traffic infringements to Director of Construction immediately
- Report unusual damage, noises, or malfunctions immediately

### Tools and Equipment
- Redemption-purchased tools for Redemption purposes only
- Report damaged or missing tools immediately
- Employees may be held responsible for negligence

## Safety and Compliance (OSHA)

- Gloves whenever handling tools, materials, or equipment
- Protective eyewear with cutting tools or impact tools
- No power tool use without demonstrated competency
- No cell phone use while operating tools or equipment
- Abide by all road laws while driving
- Report dangerous situations or accidents immediately

## Compensation & Payroll

- **Pay schedule:** Biweekly (Mon–Sun), paychecks issued following Friday
- **Timekeeping:** Clock in/out each shift; submit timesheets by Sunday 6:00 PM
- **Clock-in rules:**
  - Standard: clock in when arriving at jobsite, out when leaving
  - Jobsite >45 min away: clock in when leaving home, out when arriving home
  - Personal vehicle: clock in when leaving home (vehicle reimbursement)
  - Passengers: clock in on arrival (exception: PM doing PM work as passenger may clock in from home)
- **Overtime:** Required in rare exceptions only; 1.5x rate over 40 hours
- **Receipts/Expenses:** Submit within 1 week per credit card policy above

### Benefits
- No employer-provided health insurance — portion of wage dedicated to employee subsidizing their own
- No retirement matching
- Biweekly pay, direct deposit available

### Bonuses
- KPI-based; different per position
- Profit sharing paid out 30 days after project completion, after callback stage is complete and customer has paid all invoices
- Employee's responsibility to remind GM to distribute bonuses
